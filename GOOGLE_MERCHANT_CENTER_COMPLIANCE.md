## üìã Product Feed Requirements (Checklist)

### Required Product Attributes
- [ ] **ID** - Unique product identifier (max 50 chars)
- [ ] **Title** - Clear product name (max 150 chars)
- [ ] **Description** - Detailed product information (max 5000 chars)
- [ ] **Link** - Product page URL (HTTPS required)
- [ ] **Image Link** - Main product image (HTTPS, min 300x300px)
- [ ] **Price** - In GBP with VAT included
- [ ] **Availability** - in_stock, out_of_stock, preorder, backorder
- [ ] **Brand** - Manufacturer/Brand name (required for new products)
- [ ] **Condition** - new, refurbished, or used
- [ ] **GTIN/EAN** - Product barcode (13-digit format)

### Recommended Product Attributes
- [ ] **Shipping** - Delivery time, cost, country coverage
- [ ] **Shipping Weight** - For postage calculations
- [ ] **Material** - Product composition
- [ ] **Size** - UK sizing system
- [ ] **Color** - Full color names (not hex codes)
- [ ] **Gender** - For apparel (male, female, unisex)
- [ ] **Age Group** - For children's products
- [ ] **Item Group ID** - For product variants
